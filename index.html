Why we exist
India's informal economy is worth trillions of rupees. It runs on trust, relationships, and zero accountability infrastructure.

From a doctor who refers 20 patients a week with no record, to a factory floor where batch quality depends on a handwritten log — critical professional workflows happen outside any formal system. Not because people don't care. Because no platform was built for how India actually works. Mitr-AI is that platform.
