The problem today

Referrals run on memory.
Accountability doesn't.

The informal referral economy in Indian healthcare moves thousands of patients every day — making them difficult to track transparently, acknowledge instantly, or audit systematically.

📋
Doctors have no referral record
Most referrals happen via phone or WhatsApp. Nothing is logged. Months later, there's no way to verify what was referred, to whom, or whether it was acted on.
📊
Hospitals & diagnostics run on Excel
Referral relationships are managed in spreadsheets that no one fully trusts. No analytics, no visibility on top referrers, no accountability for who's been acknowledged.
🔕
No mutual acknowledgement loop
After a referral is made, neither party has a clean way to confirm it was received, acted on, or completed. Disputes fester with no paper trail on either side.
👥
Delegation exposes too much
When a hospital owner delegates tracking to a staff member, sensitive relationship details get exposed. Operations need to be managed without revealing financial arrangements.
Built for both sides

How it works

Simple for a clinic assistant.
Powerful for a hospital's management.

📝
Log the referral
Doctor or assistant logs patient name, destination hospital or diagnostic centre, and reason. Takes under 30 seconds.
🔔
Destination notified
If the hospital or diagnostic centre is on Mitr-AI, they receive an instant notification with referral details.
🚩
Both sides flag
Doctor marks: received or pending. Hospital marks: acknowledged or raise a query. Flags are independent — neither sees the other's internal notes.
✅
Full visibility
Doctor sees a clean referral log. Hospital sees analytics. No disputes left hanging. No Excel to update.

Design principle

Built on zero trust.
No financial data. Ever.

Mitr-AI Refer solves accountability without creating financial liability or a legal paper trail. Your relationship details stay entirely outside the system.

0
Financial amounts stored
No fees, commissions, or payment amounts are logged, displayed, or transmitted on the platform — ever.
30
Day data retention on exit
When you leave Mitr-AI, your data is available for download for 30 days. After that, it is permanently deleted from our servers.
∞
Audit trail for your records
Every referral is timestamped and permanently logged. A complete history is yours — for medico-legal use, practice management, or operations.
